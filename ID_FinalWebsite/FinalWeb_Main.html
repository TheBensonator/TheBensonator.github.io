My Story

Creativity has always been something I have striven for. From a early age I grew up wanted to become a Disney Imagineer. I read every book I could find, practiced sketching, and took auto shop to round myself out.

I sought to learn anything that I could about working with my hands and working with others to make something new. This meant learning, to joining a race team and learning to weld in junior high school, working at a recording studio through high school, and eventually entering the computer programming world in college.

Through these and many more experiences I found a deep appreciation and reverence for each of the challenges I had the chance to learn from. I hope to take that combined knowledge to serve those around me and bring joy to those I can.
